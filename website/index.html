Wine and Cannabis Tour

Welcome to High Expectations Tours! Thank you for checking us out as we are just a small company from Sonoma that takes pride in taking out smaller groups to explore some of the best that Sonoma County has to offer… World Class Wines and Cannabis! If you choose to partake in a day with us, it will be a small group setting so you all will get to know one another and what better way to meet new friends over wine and Cannabis! You will not find any of the wines that you will taste back home or even locally since it will all be small batch hard to find family wineries. Your fun local guide has been sought after to join us from the wine industry so pretty much you will be hanging out with a wine geek for the day! Our mission for you is have an awesome educational fun day playing in our back yard of Sonoma County!

We would love to take you and your group out, at this time our pick up locations are located in San Francisco, so take a look at the FAQ button for time and pick up locations…It’s Union Square @9:45 and Fisherman’s Wharf @10:00. Lots of good info there but for any additional questions please give us a call and a friendly voice will be happy to have a chat with you.
